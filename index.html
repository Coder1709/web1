Introduction to the book

Battle Through the Heavens (Chinese: 斗破苍穹; pinyin: Dòupò Cāngqióng; lit.'Fight Through The Sky')
is a 2018 Chinese television series adapted from the eponymous novel Doupo Cangqiong (斗破苍穹)by Tiancan Tudou
(天蚕土豆). It stars Leo Wu, Lin Yun, Baron Chen, Li Qin, Xin Zhilei and Liu Meitong.[1]The series aired on Hunan
TV on September 3, 2018 till October 25, 2018.
